Software-defined Application Services

The Avi Vantage Platform delivers public-cloud-like agility for application services. It is architected on software-defined principles with separate central control and distributed service delivery . The platform goes beyond load balancing to provide deep application analytics, predictive autoscaling, security, and self-service for app owners on-premises or in the cloud.
