The Team

Based in Silicon Valley, the Avi Networks team brings together entrepreneurial as well as public company DNA. Before Avi, the founding team designed and managed products that generated multi-billion dollar revenues for Cisco Systems.

  • Umesh Mahajan
    CEO and Co-Founder
    A senior executive with over 25+ years of experience, Umesh has helped develop the vision, strategy and execution plan for several innovative technology products.
    Most recently, Umesh was the VP/GM for a $2B+ Data Center switching business at Cisco leading engineering, product management and operations for Nexus 7000 and MDS switching products and the NX-OS operating system. Prior to this, Umesh led the software team at Andiamo in architecting and delivering SAN-OS. Umesh has a Masters in CS from Duke and a Bachelors from IIT Delhi and has over 26 patents to date.

  • Murali Basavaiah
    VP Engineering and Co-Founder
    With 20+ years of expertise in Distributed Systems, Networking, and Storage, Murali was most recently the VP of Software Engineering for the Data Center group at Cisco, having joined Cisco through the acquisition of Andiamo.
    Prior to Cisco, Murali was Chief Architect at Shasta Networks (acquired by Nortel), where he led the architecture for the innovative Shasta Broadband IP Services Platform, a distributed multi-subscriber services engine. Murali has a Masters in CS from Arizona State University, a certificate from the Stanford Executive Program and over 20 patents to date.

  • Ranga Rajagopalan
    CTO and Co-Founder
    Ranga has architected and developed several high performance distributed operating systems, networking, and storage data center products over the last 15 years.

He joined Cisco through the acquisition of Andiamo where he was one of the lead architects for SAN-OS operating system. Our Investors

Our investors include two of the top infrastructure investors today, particularly in networking, Greylock Partners and Lightspeed Venture Partners. Their combined experience and guidance keep us focused on the ultimate goal - Build the next major Silicon Valley based network infrastructure company!

  • Greylock partners with game-changing entrepreneurs to build market-transforming companies. Our partner companies have made 150 public offerings and more than 100 of our companies have achieved profitable M&A events. Greylock-backed companies include Facebook (NASDAQ: FB), Imperva (NYSE: IMPV) LinkedIn (NYSE:LNKD), Palo Alto Networks (NYSE: PANW), Pandora (NYSE), Workday (NYSE: WDAY), Airbnb, Apptio, Cloudera, Coupons.com, Dropbox, One Kings Lane, Redfin, and tumblr. Greylock was founded in 1965 and has $2 billion under management. The headquarters are in Silicon Valley with offices in Cambridge, Beijing and Bangalore.
